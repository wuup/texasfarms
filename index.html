Our Passion, Your Plate

At Texas Farms Vermont, we're driven by a passion for creating the highest quality, farm-fresh products. Our commitment to sustainable farming practices and animal welfare ensures that every bite tells a story of care, dedication, and respect for nature.

Sustainable Practices

We employ eco-friendly farming methods that protect our land and resources for future generations.

Animal Welfare

Our animals are raised with love and respect, ensuring their well-being and the quality of our products.

Community Focus

We're proud to support our local community by providing fresh and nutritious food.

Goats

Our Nubian goats are raised in a natural, free-range environment, providing high-quality milk and meat products.

We take pride in ensuring our goats are healthy and happy, which in turn produces the best quality products for our customers. Our goats are fed a balanced diet and have plenty of space to roam and graze.

Pigs

We specialize in rare breed Gloucestershire Old Spot pigs, known for their exceptional meat quality and ethical upbringing.

Our pigs are raised in a stress-free environment, with access to outdoor spaces and natural foraging opportunities. This not only ensures their well-being but also results in superior meat quality that is rich in flavor and texture.

Chickens

Our Chantecler chickens produce delicious, nutrient-rich eggs and tender meat, raised without antibiotics or hormones.

We believe in raising our chickens in a natural and humane way. They have access to open spaces and are free to roam, which contributes to their overall health and the quality of the eggs and meat they produce.

Vegetables

Fresh, organically grown vegetables harvested at peak ripeness to ensure the best taste and nutritional value.

Our vegetables are grown using sustainable farming practices, without the use of synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We focus on soil health and biodiversity to produce the freshest and most nutritious vegetables possible.
