Our Heritage Animals

We specialize in raising heritage breed animals, preserving genetic diversity while producing exceptional quality products.

Gloucestershire Old Spot Pigs

The Gloucestershire Old Spot pig is the oldest pedigreed spotted pig in the world. Once popular on small family farms where they were raised on dropped orchard fruit, earning them the nicknames of the "orchard pig" or "cottage pig." This breed is known for being excellent foragers, attentive mothers, and their gentle temperaments.

The richly marbled meat has a depth of flavor that is completely unmatched. It's no surprise this breed is the favorite of the British Royalty!

We sell feeder piglets as well as registered GOS breeding stock. See the farm store for pricing on half and whole shares as well as individual retail cuts.

Nubian Goats

The Nubian goat is a large, graceful breed known for its long, floppy ears and Roman nose. This breed is highly valued for its high-quality milk, which has a higher butterfat content compared to other dairy goats, making it ideal for cheese and butter production.

Our Nubian goats are raised in a natural, free-range environment, providing them with plenty of space to roam and graze. We take pride in ensuring our goats are healthy and happy, which in turn produces the best quality milk and meat products for our customers.

Speckled Sussex Chickens

The Speckled Sussex is a heritage breed chicken known for its beautiful plumage and excellent dual-purpose characteristics. These friendly birds are exceptional foragers and adapt well to free-range environments.

Our chickens produce delicious, nutrient-rich eggs and tender meat, raised without antibiotics or hormones. We believe in raising our chickens in a natural and humane way.

Learn More About Our Chickens

Champagne D'Argent Rabbits

The Champagne D'Argent is one of the oldest rabbit breeds, originating in France. Known for their striking silver coat that develops with age, these rabbits are prized for both their beautiful appearance and excellent meat quality.

Our rabbits are raised in spacious, clean environments with plenty of enrichment activities. We focus on breeding for both show quality and meat production.

Learn More About Our Rabbits

Toulouse Geese

The Toulouse goose is a heritage breed known for its calm temperament and impressive size. Originally from France, these geese are excellent foragers and natural lawn maintainers.

Our Toulouse geese are raised free-range, contributing to our sustainable farming practices by helping with weed control and soil fertilization.
