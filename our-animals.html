Heritage Animals
Raised with Care

We preserve rare heritage breeds while producing exceptional quality pork, eggs, rabbit, and more—all raised on pasture in Concord, Vermont.

Our commitment to heritage breeds means raising animals the way they were meant to live—outdoors, with space to forage, root, and express natural behaviors. The result is healthier animals and better-tasting food.

Heritage Pigs

Gloucestershire Old Spot Pigs

The oldest pedigreed spotted pig in the world, once popular on small family farms earning them the nickname "orchard pig." Known for excellent foraging, attentive mothers, gentle temperaments, and richly marbled meat with unmatched flavor.

We sell feeder piglets, registered breeding stock, and pork by the half, whole, or individual cuts—plus artisan charcuterie.

Heritage Rabbits

Champagne D'Argent Rabbits

One of the oldest rabbit breeds, originating in France over 400 years ago. Known for their striking silver coat that develops from jet black as they mature, these rabbits are prized for both their beauty and excellent meat quality.

We sell breeding stock—junior does, junior bucks, and proven breeders—with full pedigrees.

Heritage Poultry

Speckled Sussex Chickens

A heritage breed known for beautiful plumage and excellent dual-purpose characteristics. These friendly birds are exceptional foragers and adapt well to free-range environments.

Our chickens produce delicious, nutrient-rich eggs and tender meat, raised without antibiotics or hormones in a natural setting.

Heritage Waterfowl

Toulouse Geese

A heritage breed known for its calm temperament and impressive size. Originally from France, these geese are excellent foragers and natural lawn maintainers.

Our Toulouse geese are raised free-range, contributing to sustainable farming through weed control and soil fertilization.
