Our Heritage Animals

We raise Vermont heritage breed animals, preserving genetic diversity while producing exceptional quality pork, eggs, and more.

Heritage Poultry

Speckled Sussex Chickens

A heritage breed known for beautiful plumage and excellent dual-purpose characteristics. These friendly birds are exceptional foragers and adapt well to free-range environments.

Our chickens produce delicious, nutrient-rich eggs and tender meat, raised without antibiotics or hormones in a natural and humane way.

Heritage Rabbits

Champagne D'Argent Rabbits

One of the oldest rabbit breeds, originating in France. Known for their striking silver coat that develops with age, these rabbits are prized for both their beautiful appearance and excellent meat quality.

Our rabbits are raised in spacious, clean environments with plenty of enrichment. We breed for both show quality and meat production.

Heritage Waterfowl

Toulouse Geese

A heritage breed known for its calm temperament and impressive size. Originally from France, these geese are excellent foragers and natural lawn maintainers.

Our Toulouse geese are raised free-range, contributing to sustainable farming through weed control and soil fertilization.
