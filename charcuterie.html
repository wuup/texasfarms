The Difference

Why Heritage Pork Makes Better Charcuterie

Gloucestershire Old Spots are known for their exceptional fat quality and marbling—the key to great charcuterie. Our pigs roam on pasture, developing complex flavors that industrial pork simply can't match.

  • Higher fat content for silky, rich texture
  • Deep, complex flavor from pasture diet
  • Traditional breeds used by artisan curers for centuries
  • No antibiotics or hormones, ever
