About the Breed

The Oldest Pedigreed Spotted Pig in the World

Gloucestershire Old Spots hail from Gloucestershire, England, where they were once popular on small family farms raised on dropped orchard fruit — earning them the nicknames "orchard pig" and "cottage pig." The richly marbled meat has a depth of flavor that is completely unmatched. It's no surprise this breed is the favorite of British Royalty!

This heritage breed is considered "Threatened" by the Livestock Conservancy with fewer than 1,000 annual registrations in the US and an estimated global population of less than 5,000. By buying and enjoying pork from Texas Farms, you are helping us continue to preserve this amazing breed.

How Our Salami Is Made

1

Pasture-Raised

Our GOS pigs forage on Vermont pastures, building flavor through a natural, stress-free life outdoors.

2

Butchered Whole

We use the whole animal with respect. Choice cuts are selected and hand-trimmed for salami production.

3

Seasoned & Stuffed

Ground with imported smoked paprika, garlic, and spices, then stuffed into natural casings by hand.

4

Slow-Cured

Hung to cure for weeks in controlled conditions until the flavors concentrate and the texture becomes silky.
