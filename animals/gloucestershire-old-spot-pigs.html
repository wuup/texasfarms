The Breed

Why Gloucestershire Old Spots?

Known as the "orchard pig," this heritage breed has been prized for centuries. Once popular on small English farms where they foraged on fallen fruit, they're now a favorite of British Royalty.

At Texas Farms Vermont, we raise GOS pigs on pasture in the Northeast Kingdom for flavorful, Vermont heritage pork.

  • Richly marbled meat with unmatched depth of flavor
  • Excellent foragers that thrive on pasture
  • Gentle temperament, easy to handle
  • Heritage genetics we've carefully maintained
