Toulouse Geese

The Gentle Giants of Waterfowl

About the Breed

The Toulouse Goose is a heritage French breed known for its calm temperament and impressive size.

Originally from the Toulouse region of France, these large, stately geese are known for their gentle nature and excellent foraging abilities. They have a distinctive dewlap under their bill and loose skin folds on their abdomen, giving them a dignified appearance. Our Toulouse geese help maintain our pastures while providing eggs and meat for the farm.

Breed Characteristics

Gentle Giants

Known for their calm demeanor and impressive size, making them excellent farm guardians.

Cold Hardy

Well-adapted to Vermont's climate with their dense down feathers.

Life on Our Farm

Daily Life

Our Toulouse geese spend their days grazing in the pastures and enjoying our pond. They have access to shelter but prefer to be outdoors in most weather conditions.

Breeding Program

We maintain a small breeding flock to preserve the heritage characteristics of the breed while selecting for hardiness in our climate.

Sustainable Practices

Our geese contribute to pasture management through grazing and provide natural fertilizer, supporting our sustainable farming practices.
