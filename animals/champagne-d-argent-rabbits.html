The Breed

About Champagne D'Argent

The Champagne D'Argent rabbit is a French heritage breed prized for its meat and pelts. First recorded in France during the 1600s, it's one of the oldest meat rabbit breeds. They have calm temperaments, good mothering instincts, and fast growth rates. Kits are born completely black, gradually turning silver—showing at 4 weeks and fully silver by 5 to 6 months.
