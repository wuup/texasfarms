Nubian Goats

Gentle Giants with Sweet Milk

We raise purebred ADGA registered Nubian goats (the ones with the floppy ears). They are known for their rich, sweet tasting milk which is perfect for cheesemaking. All of our goats are well socialized and easy to handle.

We are a CAE-free tested herd. Our herd was added to the farm in 2023 and we're expecting our first babies in 2025.
