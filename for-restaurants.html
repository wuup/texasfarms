The Heritage Difference

Why Chefs Choose GOS Pork

Gloucestershire Old Spots are one of the world's oldest pedigreed pig breeds. Known as "orchard pigs," they were prized on English farms for centuries for their foraging ability and exceptional meat quality.

The difference is on the plate: rich marbling, depth of flavor, and a texture that industrial pork simply cannot match.

  • Superior marbling for moisture and flavor
  • Fat that renders beautifully for cooking
  • Heritage story that resonates with diners
  • Supporting regenerative, local agriculture
